Award List System - Basic Info

Mark Criteria (Final):
  • KG: Total 500 Marks
  • 1st, 2nd, 3rd: Total 700 Marks
  • 4th, 5th: Total 800 Marks
Semester Mode:
  • 1st Semester: Auto-calculates 45% of marks entered.
  • Final Mode: Takes full marks (100) for calculation.
Passing & Locks:
  • Passing: Automatic Red Fail at < 33%.
  • Locks: Non-applicable subjects are locked.
Data Saving:
  • Click SAVE DATA to store information in this browser.
  • Data stays even if you close the window.
Delete Information:
  • Single Student: Click the Red X at the start of any row.
  • Entire Class: Click RESET ALL in the top bar to reset the current class.
